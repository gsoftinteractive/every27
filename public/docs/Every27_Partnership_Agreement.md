**WHEREAS**:

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:

**1.1** "**Access Fee**" means the monthly fee of ₦1,500 (One Thousand Five Hundred Naira) per employee for access to the Every27 platform.

**1.2** "**Company Wallet**" means the digital account maintained by Every27 on behalf of the Company for the purpose of holding funds for payroll disbursement.

**1.3** "**Employee**" means any individual employed by the Company and registered on the Every27 platform for salary disbursement.

**1.4** "**Employee Wallet**" means the digital account maintained by Every27 on behalf of each Employee for receiving salary payments.

**1.5** "**Payday**" or "**The 27th**" means the 27th day of each calendar month when salary disbursements are processed.

**1.6** "**Platform**" means the Every27 web application and associated services accessible at every27.com.

**1.7** "**Salary Advance**" means the facility allowing Employees to access up to 75% of their monthly salary before Payday.

**1.8** "**Transaction Fee**" means the fee charged on Salary Advances, set at a default rate of 7% or as otherwise agreed in writing.

**1.9** "**Working Day**" means any day other than Saturday, Sunday, or public holidays in Nigeria.

## ARTICLE 2: SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 2.1 Services Provided by Every27

Every27 agrees to provide the Company with the following services:

a) **Platform Access**: Secure access to the Every27 payroll management platform for the Company and its Employees.

b) **Employee Management**: Tools for adding, managing, and deactivating employee records on the platform.

c) **Wallet Management**: Digital wallet facilities for both Company and Employee fund management.

d) **Automated Payroll Processing**: Automatic disbursement of salaries to Employee Wallets on the 27th of each month.

e) **Salary Adjustments**: Facility to add bonuses, deductions, and allowances to employee records.

f) **Salary Advance Facility**: Subject to eligibility criteria, provision of salary advance services to Employees.

g) **Withdrawal Services**: Facility for Employees to withdraw funds from their wallets to their personal bank accounts.

h) **Reporting**: Transaction history, payroll summaries, and relevant reports.

i) **Notifications**: Email alerts for significant platform activities.

j) **Customer Support**: Reasonable customer support during business hours.

### 2.2 Services NOT Included

The following are expressly excluded from this Agreement:

a) Tax calculations, filings, or compliance (Company's responsibility)
b) Pension deductions and remittances (Company's responsibility)
c) Human resource management services
d) Employee recruitment or verification
e) Legal or accounting advice
f) Physical cash disbursement

### 5.1 Access Fee

a) The Company shall pay an Access Fee of **₦1,500 (One Thousand Five Hundred Naira)** per Employee per month.

b) Access Fees are deducted automatically from the Company Wallet on Payday (the 27th).

c) Access Fees are non-refundable once deducted.

d) Access Fees apply to all active Employees on the Platform as of the 27th.

### 5.2 Transaction Fee (Salary Advance)

## ARTICLE 6: SALARY ADVANCE TERMS

### 6.1 Eligibility

Employees are eligible for Salary Advance if:

a) They have been registered on the Platform for at least **ONE (1) CALENDAR MONTH**.

b) The Company has enabled the Salary Advance feature.

c) The Employee has not been blocked from Salary Advances by the Company.

d) The Employee's total outstanding advances do not exceed **75% (Seventy-Five Percent)** of their monthly salary.

### 6.2 Advance Limits

a) Maximum Salary Advance: **75%** of Employee's monthly salary.

b) Multiple advances are permitted within the 75% limit.

c) Maximum total liability (advance + fee): **80.25%** of monthly salary.

### 6.3 Repayment

a) Salary Advances are repaid automatically on Payday (the 27th).

b) The full amount (advance + Transaction Fee) is deducted from the Employee's salary.

c) No partial repayments are available.

### 6.4 Company Acknowledgment

The Company acknowledges that:

a) Salary Advances are provided to Employees at Every27's discretion.

b) Every27 bears the credit risk for Salary Advances.

c) The Company shall cooperate in recovering advances from terminated Employees.

## ARTICLE 7: PAYROLL PROCESSING

### 7.1 Processing Schedule

a) Payroll is processed on the **27th of each calendar month**.

b) Processing occurs regardless of whether the 27th falls on a weekend or public holiday.

c) Processing follows this order:
   1. Deduction of Access Fees from Company Wallet
   2. Verification of remaining balance for salaries
   3. For each Employee: Deduction of Salary Advance owing (if any)
   4. Credit of net salary to Employee Wallet

### 7.2 Insufficient Funds

If the Company Wallet has insufficient funds on Payday:

a) Access Fees will still be deducted.

b) Salaries will **NOT** be processed.

c) The Company will receive an email notification.

d) Employees will be notified of the delay.

e) The Company must fund the wallet and contact Every27 for manual processing.

### 7.3 No Partial Payments

Every27 does not process partial salary payments. Either all Employees are paid, or none are paid.

### 11.1 Term

### 11.2 Termination by Either Party

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing **THIRTY (30) DAYS** written notice to the other party.

### 11.3 Termination by Every27 (Immediate)

Every27 may terminate this Agreement immediately if:

a) The Company breaches any material term of this Agreement.

b) The Company fails to fund its wallet for THREE (3) consecutive months.

c) The Company engages in fraudulent or illegal activities.

d) The Company becomes insolvent or enters bankruptcy proceedings.

### 11.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:

a) The Company's access to the Platform will be suspended.

b) Employees' access will be terminated.

c) Outstanding Salary Advances must be repaid or the Company must make arrangements for recovery.

d) Any remaining balance in the Company Wallet (after deducting fees and outstanding amounts) will be refunded within **FOURTEEN (14) DAYS**.

e) Employee Wallet balances must be withdrawn by Employees within **THIRTY (30) DAYS**, after which unclaimed funds may be forfeited.
