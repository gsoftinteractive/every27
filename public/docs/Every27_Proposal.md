# The Challenge

Payroll management remains one of the biggest operational challenges for businesses in Nigeria. Companies struggle with manual processes that lead to costly errors and unhappy employees.

### Late Salary Payments
Employees suffer when salaries don't arrive on time, affecting their financial planning and causing workplace dissatisfaction.

### Manual Calculations
Spreadsheet-based payroll leads to calculation errors, tax mistakes, and compliance issues that cost businesses money.

### Cash Flow Management
Poor visibility into payroll obligations makes it difficult to plan finances and ensure funds are available on payday.

### Employee Financial Stress
Without access to earned wages, employees resort to expensive loans and predatory lending to meet urgent financial needs.

**Statistics:**
- 67% of employees affected by late pay
- 40+ hours spent on monthly manual processing
- 25% payroll error rate in manual systems

# How It Works

### Step 1: Onboard Your Company
Submit your CAC registration documents and company details. Our team verifies your information and sets up your company portal within 24-48 hours.

### Step 2: Add Your Employees
Add employees individually or use our bulk import feature with CSV files. Set salaries, allowances, bonuses, and deductions for each employee.

### Step 3: Fund Your Wallet
Top up your company wallet via bank transfer or online payment. Ensure sufficient funds are available before the 27th for seamless payroll processing.

### Step 4: Automatic Payday on the 27th
On the 27th of every month, salaries are automatically calculated and credited to employee wallets. Employees receive instant notifications and can withdraw to their bank accounts.
